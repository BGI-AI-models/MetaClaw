# Experimental Design Patterns

Use this reference to propose experiments that can distinguish between competing hypotheses.

## Common patterns
- dose-response studies
- gain/loss of function studies
- time-course studies
- between-subjects experiments
- repeated-measures designs
- factorial designs
- in silico simulations
- observational studies
- cohort studies
- case-control studies
- randomized controlled trials
- crossover trials

## What to specify
For each proposed test, state:
- what is measured
- what the control is
- what comparison matters
- what sample or data source is needed
- what result would support or falsify the hypothesis

## Practical guidance
Choose the simplest design that can distinguish between the competing explanations.
