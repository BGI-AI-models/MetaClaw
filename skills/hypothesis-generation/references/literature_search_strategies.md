# Literature Search Strategies

Use this reference to ground hypotheses in evidence before proposing new explanations.

## Search approach
1. Start broad to identify the landscape.
2. Narrow to specific mechanisms, pathways, or theories.
3. Mine references and related papers from the strongest sources.

## Good search targets
- reviews
- systematic reviews
- meta-analyses
- primary mechanistic studies
- methodological papers
- analogous systems in related domains

## What to capture
- key findings
- study design
- limitations
- conflicting evidence
- gaps that remain unresolved

## Search discipline
Do not rely on only supportive sources. Actively look for contradictory evidence and unresolved debates.
